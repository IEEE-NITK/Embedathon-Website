What is Embedathon?

Embedathon is a 3-week long embedded systems task-based hackathon challenge hosted by IEEE NITK student branch in association with CAS Society. It presents you with interesting problem statements to ensure a fun-filled learning experience. This is the fourth edition of the event promising to be bigger and better. In addition to the problem statement resources, workshops and talks will also be conducted prior to the final event to enhance your experience with us! The expert talk will be open to everyone, even if you did not qualify the preliminary round.

Open to 1st and 2nd year students from all over India!
