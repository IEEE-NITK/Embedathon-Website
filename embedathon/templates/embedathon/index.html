What is Embedathon?

Embedathon is our flagship embedded systems task-based hackathon challenge hosted by IEEE NITK student branch in association with CAS Society. It presents you with interesting problem statements to ensure a fun-filled learning experience. This is the fifth edition of the event promising to be bigger and better. In addition to the problem statement resources, workshops and talks will also be conducted prior to the final event to enhance your experience with us! The expert talk will be open to everyone, even if you did not qualify the preliminary round.

Open to 1st and 2nd year students from all over India!

Frequently Asked Questions

This is going to be a pan-India event so all the 1st and 2nd Year B.E/B.Tech/B.Sc students from any college can register for the event.

Free registrations for teams that have at least one IEEE student member, and for all teams comprising students from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal.
Registration fee of Rs. 150/- for teams with no IEEE student members or do not belong to the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal.

It is not necessary to have any previous background in EEE or ECE. Students who are curious about Embedded Systems and are interested to learn and get hands-on experience are welcome to apply.

Students can apply individually or in a team of 2. Only one hardware kit will be provided to each team.

Round 1 online test would be conducted over HackerEarth. The test would be covering topics such as aptitude and basic electronics.

Hardware kits would be made available to the students outside NITK via courier delivery. We expect kits to be handled very carefully throughout the competition and sent back after the completion of it.

Students will have to arrange for their own accommodation. Snacks and refreshments would be provided on that day.

Brush up on your knowledge of basics of popular microcontrollers, and basic electronics.
